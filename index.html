Clint Borgen

Founder & President, The Borgen Project

Clint Borgen is an American activist and the founder and president of The Borgen Project, a non-profit organization that works to fight global poverty. The project tries to convince the U.S. government to support people living in extreme poverty around the world.

Clint became interested in this work after visiting refugee camps in Kosovo in 1999. He saw how difficult life was for people there and decided to take action.

After university, he worked on a fishing boat in Alaska to save money and start The Borgen Project in 2003. From those humble beginnings in one of Earth's most remote locations, the organization grew into a powerful force for change.

Today, his organization has many volunteers across the U.S. and works with politicians to support laws that help people in need. Clint believes that everyone can help make the world a better place, even with small actions.

Clint Borgen – Bucha, Ukraine – February 2023

Key facts

  • Volunteered in Kosovo refugee camps, 1999
